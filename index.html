Project 01: Manifesto

Nine Iterations

Follow the Rhythm

Taking your favourite song you are required to present the lyrics in a manner which is appropriate to the music and singer/group in question. Consider the pacing and rhythm of the song. If lines or words are more meaningful than others they should be presented more prominently in your final piece.

Your research should look at styles of typography you feel will be most suitable for the music genre in question. This could include concrete poetry to illustrate how the text might be presented.
